 Michael Joseph Jackson was the eighth child of the Jackson family . Known as the "King of Pop", he is regarded as one of the most significant cultural figures of the 20th century . During his four-decade career, his contributions to music, dance, and fashion, along with his publicized personal life, made him a global figure in popular culture . His music videos, including those for "Beat It", "Billie Jean", and "Thriller", are credited with breaking racial barriers and transforming the medium into an art form .
